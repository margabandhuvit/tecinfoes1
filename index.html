Harmonized System of Nomenclature (HSN) / HS Codes

HSN codes are internationally recognized alphanumeric identifiers used to classify automotive spare parts. They ensure systematic categorization, streamline trade, simplify customs procedures, and reduce ambiguity in cross-border transactions.

These codes play a vital role in inventory management and maintaining transparency across the global supply chain. Every component is uniquely identified with its own HS code, promoting clarity and regulatory compliance.

Understanding and properly applying these codes is critical for businesses aiming to optimize operations and meet international trade requirements.

Know More

Our team has extensive experience in assigning accurate HS codes to automotive components. We help OEMs improve visibility in their import/export processes and ensure regulatory alignment across international markets.

Right to Repair (R2R)

Right to Repair is a legal and ethical initiative empowering consumers and third-party repair providers to service, repair, and maintain their products independently—without reliance on OEMs. This movement is rapidly gaining legal traction across global markets.

Tecinfo supports OEMs in building compliant R2R documentation, designing portals, and creating frameworks that simplify self-service and third-party repair processes for end users.

By enabling transparent access to parts, tools, and information, OEMs can reduce aftersales overhead, build customer trust, and remain compliant with evolving R2R laws. It’s not just a legal requirement—it’s a path to greater customer satisfaction and product longevity.

Know More
  • Guidance to design teams for repair-friendly product architecture
  • Strategies for ensuring availability of spare parts and tools
  • Clear documentation for Software Over-the-Air (SOTA) and Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA) updates
  • Support in creating R2R portals and maintaining repair documentation
  • Compliance support with global Right to Repair regulations
  • Design practices that allow easy disassembly and repair
  • Access to software tools and diagnostic information under fair market conditions
